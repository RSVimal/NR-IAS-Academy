Our Founder

Founder of NR IS ACADEMY is R.VIJAYALAYAN,M.Sc.(Agri).,PGDCA.He named this institute NR in memory of his father N.Rthinsamy.

R.VIJAYALAYAN is the Director of our Institute.
The Academy was established with the aim to provide a complete solution to the students preparing for these exams here in and around Trichy with dedication and to a excellent quality at par and above any institution of its kind at Chennai or Delhi.

NR IAS ACADEMY was started in November - 2003.
Till now we have created 35 IAS,72 IPS,63 IRS,25 IFS Officers in UPSC exams.

Many of the schools in Tamilnadu will have student of NR TRB academt as their Elementary,UG and PG Teachers who cleared TRB through our guidance and Coaching.
We are specialist in History optional paper and General studies in UPSC Exam.We are having Excellant Tamil materiaals for UPSC History Mains and General studies Mains paper.
Our Tamil Version of study materials published by Adhi Publications,Trichy are very popular all over Tamilnadu.
We are equipped with very hard working,excellent faculty,highly experienced in coching UPSC and TNPSC ,TET Exams over sevral years. Our Class rooms are equipped with state of art audio,visual support and LCD projectors.
