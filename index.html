Getting Started

  • 1)Use your roll no. to register with the app.
  • 2)Please use a valid email address that you have access to.
  • 3)Navigate through the side bar to access different features of the app.
  • 4)You can choose to upgrade to Student Plus plan by navigating to the Live section.
  • 5)You will be prompted to make a payment of Rs.1000.
  • 6)On completing the payment, you can access live streams and on demand videos.
  • 7)The plan will be automatically downgraded after 30 days from the day of payment.

What Do You Get From NR Student App

  • An exclusive app for the students of NA IAS Academy, to help them practice effectively.
  • Students can take their practice tests online and keep track of their scores.
  • Students can access study materials and model questions.
  • Get notifications from staff/management
  • Access online sessions streamed live from the institution.
  • Access on demand videos of previous recorded classroom sessions.
